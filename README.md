# ULTRAKILL-Additions-I-Want
Currently, this repository just acts as a main repository for me to list all of my ULTRAKILL mods in one place.
However, in the future I intend to commit all of the mod's source code as git submodules or something.
But for now, here's the list of mods!

- [NyxLib](https://github.com/nyxpirientity/ULTRAKILL-NyxLib) - main library used by my mods
- [Energy Control](https://github.com/nyxpirientity/ULTRAKILL-EnergyControl) - adds an energy control monobehaviour to the player, letting other mods control energy costs and probably other stuff
- [Hydra Enemies](https://github.com/nyxpirientity/ULTRAKILL-HydraEnemies) - enemy modifier that causes enemies to split when killed, and those cloned splits must be killed quickly for the enemy to truly die
- [Mundane Murder](https://github.com/nyxpirientity/ULTRAKILL-MundaneMurder) - terrible. inverses the style system.
- [Pain Tracker](https://github.com/nyxpirientity/ULTRAKILL-PainTracker) - tracks the inflicted pain of every enemy in the game, and you, and combines it into one value mostly a dependency for pain based mod(s) of mine
- [Enemy Friends](https://github.com/nyxpirientity/ULTRAKILL-EnemyFriends) - gives enemies a certain amount of friends when they spawn in, depending on configuration
- [Blood Fueled Enemies](https://github.com/nyxpirientity/ULTRAKILL-BloodFueledEnemies) - enemies heal from YOUR blood, aka, when you get hit, enemies within range heal
- [Heat of Heck](https://github.com/nyxpirientity/ULTRAKILL-HeatOfHeck) - causes an internal temperature meter to appear at the top of your screen, which causes numerous effects, some negative, some positive, depending on how low your internal temperature is. The meter is configurable based on your *style rank*, allowing it to be more aggressive when you're stylish for example (default behaviour)
- [Salty Enemies](https://github.com/nyxpirientity/ULTRAKILL-SaltyEnemies) - enemies get very VERY angry when you do stylishly, or, however you configure it. Attaches style to radiance levels!
- [Self Conscience](https://github.com/nyxpirientity/ULTRAKILL-SelfConscience) - attaches energy/stamina to style rank, so by default behaviour is at destructive you can only do 2 dashes before draining your energy, whereas at ultrakill, it's normal + like, 0.05 dash energy for another dash or something idk
- [Aggressive Agony](https://github.com/nyxpirientity/ULTRAKILL-AggressiveAgony)
- [Feedbackers For All](https://github.com/nyxpirientity/ULTRAKILL-FeedbackersForAll)
- [Heck Puppets](https://github.com/nyxpirientity/ULTRAKILL-HeckPuppets)
- [Cybergrind Quick Restart](https://github.com/nyxpirientity/ULTRAKILL-CybergrindQuickRestart)
- [Cybergrind Challenger](https://github.com/nyxpirientity/ULTRAKILL-CybergrindChallenger)
- [Bad Gyro](https://github.com/nyxpirientity/ULTRAKILL-BadGyro)
- [Cyberloop](https://github.com/nyxpirientity/ULTRAKILL-Cyberloop) - adds portals to cybergrind in various ways via randomly selected presets
